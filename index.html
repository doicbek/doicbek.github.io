Dominic Beck

I'm a postdoctoral research fellow in the CMB group at Stanford University and the SLAC National Accelerator Laboratory. I analyze large astrophysical data sets to learn more about our Universe through statistical interference and high-performance computing.

I'm affiliated with the Kavli Institute for Particle Astrophysics and Cosmology and I'm part of several collaborations with the goal of make state-of-the-art measurements of the Cosmic Microwave Background, including BICEP/Keck, LiteBIRD and CMB-S4.

I did my PhD in astrophysics with Radek Stompor and Josquin Errard at the University of Paris after obtaining a double master's degree in physics from ETH Zürich and École Polytechnique Paris.
